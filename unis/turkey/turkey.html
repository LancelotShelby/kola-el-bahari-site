Study in Turkey

Turkey is a transcontinental country known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and growing economy. It's a popular destination for international students due to its affordable tuition fees and a wide range of academic programs.

Visa Information

Students from most countries will need to apply for a student visa at a Turkish embassy or consulate. A letter of final acceptance from a university is required for the application.

Cost of Living

The cost of living in Turkey is relatively low compared to many European countries. Monthly expenses for a student can range from $300 to $1000, depending on the city and lifestyle.
